    <h1>Research Focus</h1>

    <p>Stanford GPS Lab research spans GPS, GNSS, and broader position, navigation, and timing systems. The original Stanford GPS Lab research focus page organizes the work into current and continuing GPS/PNT research and early GPS/PNT research.</p>

    <p>Current research includes WAAS/SBAS, LAAS/GBAS, cyber safety for transportation, alternative PNT, time transfer, IMU development, multi-constellation GNSS, marine animal tracking, positive train control, and GNSS software receivers.</p>

    <p>Early GPS/PNT research included GPS for spacecraft control, autonomous aircraft, airplane navigation and landings, precision farming, RAIM, JPALS, eLORAN, GPS tunnel-in-the-sky concepts, and Arctic navigation.</p>
