Jason Anderson wins ION's Bradford W. Parkinson Award for his thesis: “Designing Cryptography Systems for GNSS Data and Ranging Authentication.” This award recognizes an outstanding graduate student in the field of Position, Navigation, Timing (PNT) and/or Applications.
